title: LOLA Portability for ActivityPub 0.3 available
author: Johannes Ernst
date: 2026-06-15 14:54
tags: portability, progress update
---

A [new version of the LOLA Portability for ActivityPub specification](https://swicg.github.io/activitypub-data-portability/lola.html)
is available, as Unofficial Draft 01 June 2026.

This is a minor bugfix release.

For more information about LOLA and why it matters, consider
[this post](https://dtinit.org/blog/2026/05/26/activitypub-and-account-portability)
but Aaron Ayerdis Espinoza at the Data Transfer Initiative, who is one
of the implementors of the specification.
